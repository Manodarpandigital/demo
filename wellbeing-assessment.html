Navigating your Wellbeing in 3 simple steps

Scientifically designed assessments to understand students’ career interests, strengths, & emotional well-being for informed decisions and holistic development.

Take the Wellbeing Assessment

We offer a wide range of Wellbeing assessments targeting specific issues like Attitude to Learning, Eating Attitude, Bullying, and Peer Relationships. You can choose one or multiple assessments based on your needs. Our straightforward assessments consist of simple statements as questions, with multiple-choice answers. Completing them takes just 10-15 minutes.

Get the Report

Our report delivers detailed insights regarding your responses in the selected domain. It includes an overall assessment as well as inferences regarding specific sub-sections of the domain. Results are reported in percentiles, providing a clear benchmark for comparison. This method ensures accuracy and precision, allowing you to understand how perform in that domain.

Form an Action Plan

Our clear and direct insights help students understand their strengths and limitations when it comes to aspects that influence well-being. This information can be used by students, parents, counselors or schools to devise goal oriented plans targeting specific areas to enhance well-being.

Manodarpan is a platform focused on career guidance and student well-being through science-backed psychometric assessments. It helps students understand their strengths, interests, and emotional landscape, enabling informed career choices, greater clarity, reduced stress, and confident life decisions.
